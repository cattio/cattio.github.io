To use the size calculators, you should take your measurements in whole inches using a flexible tape measure.
a: bust/chest measurement is found by measuring over the fullest part of your chest while wearing a bra that fits
b: under bust measurement is taken directly under the bust
c: waist measurement is taken around the smallest part of your waist
